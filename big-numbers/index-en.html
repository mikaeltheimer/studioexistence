A journey to the edge of
big numbers

A journey from the number 1 to the furthest edges of what the human mind has ever dared to name.

One question will follow us the whole way : how far would you have to travel to get from one number to the next ? We'll start in meters. Very soon, meters won't be enough. Then the entire universe won't be enough. And in the end : nothing will be enough — not even the numbers themselves.
